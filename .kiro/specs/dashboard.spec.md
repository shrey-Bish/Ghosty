# Social Capital Dashboard Spec

## User Story
As a user, I want to see my conference relationships as a career pipeline so that invisible social capital becomes trackable.

## Requirements (EARS)
- WHEN the dashboard opens, the app SHALL show estimated career pipeline value.
- The app SHALL show social capital growth over time.
- The app SHALL show intent tag distribution.
- The app SHALL surface at-risk high-value contacts.
- WHEN an event ends, the app SHALL generate a concise event summary.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] Pipeline value hero stat is implemented.
- [x] Timeline chart is implemented.
- [x] Intent tag pills are implemented.
- [x] At-risk banner links to follow-up queue.
- [x] Event Wrapped summary sheet is implemented.
