# QR Quick Add Spec

## User Story
As a conference attendee, I want a fast QR exchange screen so that I can share my identity before adding voice context.

## Requirements (EARS)
- WHEN the user opens QR Quick Add, the app SHALL show their Ghosty code.
- WHEN the user wants to scan another code, the app SHALL show a camera-style scan frame.
- The QR view SHALL maintain the Ghosty design language and include the user's profile identity.
- The screen SHALL include a non-camera fallback path for email entry.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] QR screen renders a styled Ghosty code.
- [x] Scan frame is visible.
- [x] Profile identity and handle are visible.
- [x] Email fallback is present.
