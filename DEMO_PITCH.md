# Ghosty — Demo Pitch Script (~400 words, ~3 minutes)

## Frame: Economics — The Transparency Guardrail

**Competing Signals: Build, Impact, Story, Collaboration**

---

**[OPEN APP — Sign In screen]**

Every conference ends the same way. You meet incredible people, exchange a few words, maybe swap a card — and within 48 hours, 80% of what made those conversations valuable is gone. The follow-up never happens. The opportunity disappears.

That's not a social problem. It's an economic one. A single recruiter connection can be worth $15K to $80K in salary negotiation. A mentor relationship can shorten a career pivot by years. But nobody tracks this. Nobody sees it. The value is invisible.

Ghosty makes it visible.

**[TAP — Profile tab]**

I sign in. My profile is ready — bio, skills, resumes, QR code. Everything a recruiter or mentor needs to remember me.

**[TAP — Events tab → View Event]**

Before the event starts, Ghosty builds my battle plan. Here's the Kiro Spark Challenge. I see every attending company — AWS, Toptal, Amazon, AI Cloud Innovation Center. Booth numbers. Who's recruiting. A personalized pitch hint for each one. I tap Summary on AWS — five mentors, what they care about, and exactly what to ask them.

**[TAP — Attend Booth on AWS → Voice Note → Record]**

After talking to Aditya Challa, I tap Attend Booth and record a 15-second voice memo. Ghosty transcribes it with Whisper, extracts structured data with Claude — name, role, intent, key details — and generates a contact card with a transparent Connection Value Score.

**[TAP — Follow Up tab → Brian Eisenlauer]**

Here's the transparency guardrail in action. Every contact has a score from 1 to 10 with a full breakdown — role seniority, company tier, career relevance, intent type, recency. Plus a salary band estimate. Brian Eisenlauer scores 9.1 — he's hiring at AWS. Ghosty has already drafted a personalized LinkedIn message referencing our actual conversation. I tap "Make shorter" — Claude rewrites it in real time. Not a template. Specific.

**[TAP — Wand tab → type "Find software roles"]**

The Magic Wand searches my entire network. I type "Find software roles" and get ranked matches with scores. Or I tap a referral prompt — "Who can introduce me to a hiring manager?" — and Ghosty finds the warm path.

**[TAP — QR tab]**

QR Quick Add lets me swap identity in one scan.

**[CLOSE]**

Ghosty turns a $0 hallway conversation into a $260K career pipeline — and makes every step of that value chain transparent. Built spec-first with Kiro in under 24 hours. Never let a great conversation ghost you.
